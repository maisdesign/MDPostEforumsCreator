Welcome to MaisDesign Post & Forums Creator.

The idea is really simple, sometimes we have users who are too lazy to do things. With this plugin they'll be able to create WordPress posts and BBPress forums only inserting their title in the form. Then they'll have to create the content but posts and forums will be ready in Draft status.
